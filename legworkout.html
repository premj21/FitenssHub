Leg Workout

Back squat

  • 1.Place barbell with weights onto the squat rack.
  • 2. Bring your body under the bar.Rest barbell over your traps and shoulders.
  • 3.Keep your feet in the same position as they would be for an air squat.
  • 4. Brace your midline and keep your chest up.

Pull-Up

  • 1. Use an overhand grip to hold the bar at hip level.
  • 2.Draw your shoulders back and keep your spine straight.
  • 3.Push your hips back as you slowly lower the bar toward your feet.
  • 4.Press your hips forward to come into a standing position with the barbell in front of your thighs.

Reverse lunges

  • 1. Take a large step backward with your left foot.
  • 2. Lower your hips so that your right thigh (front leg) becomes parallel to the floor with your right knee positioned directly over your ankle.
  • 3. our left knee should be bent at a 90-degree angle and pointing toward the floor with your left heel lifted.
