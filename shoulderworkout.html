Shoulder Workout

Barbell Standing Press

  • 1.Place barbell with weights onto the squat rack.
  • 2. Bring your body under the bar.Rest barbell over your traps and shoulders.
  • 3.Keep your feet in the same position as they would be for an air squat.
  • 4. Brace your midline and keep your chest up.

Lateral Raise

  • 1. Stand or sit with a dumbbell in each hand at your sides.
  • 2.Keep your back straight, brace your core, and thenslowly lift the weights out the side until your arms are parallel to the floor.
  • 3.Then lower them back down, again in measured fashion – you’ll find it all the harder if you avoid speeding up.

Reverse lunges

  • 1. Breathe in and brace the abdominals. Keep your back straight, chest up, and eyes focused forward.
  • 2. Lift the barbell up (toward the chin) as you exhale.
  • 3.Pause at the top of the lift.Lower the barbell as you inhale, returning it to the starting position.
